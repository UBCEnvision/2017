The Patri-Car-Key

The Patri-Car-Key is a zinc-air powered vehicle that uses an iodine clock timing reaction. The vehicle is actuated with an Arduino controller that has custom electronics and an algorithm designed to reduce operational errors. Safe operation is emphasized in the design features.

Mechanical Design

Secure suspension ensure consistent steering. High-traction wheels prevent slipping.

Integrated Water Tank

Power Source

The motor runs on zinc-air batteries.

Electrochemistry

Oxidation of zinc on the anode releases electrons which pass through an external circuit and travel to the cathode where oxygen is reduced to hydroxide ions.

Electrical

An Arduino microcontroller is the brain of the car. It collects sensor readings, performs calculations, drives motors and transmits data.

Stopping Mechanism

We use an iodine clock.
